## Overview

Personal newsletter aggregator and podcast generator. Fetches newsletters from Gmail, uses an LLM to filter and summarise stories based on your interests, produces a daily digest, and optionally generates a TTS podcast episode.## Features

## Features
- Connect your Gmail account to fetch newsletters
- Use an LLM to filter and summarise stories based on your interests
- Produce a daily digest of relevant stories
- Optionally generate a TTS podcast episode of the digest
- Web interface to view digests, listen to podcasts, and manage settings
- Listen to your personalised podcasts in your favourite podcast app via RSS feed
